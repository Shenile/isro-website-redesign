Historic 100th Space Launch GSLV-F15/NVS-02

ISRO marked a major milestone on January 24, 2025, with its 100th launch from Sriharikota, deploying the NVS-02 navigation satellite via GSLV-F15. This strengthens the NavIC system, enhancing positioning, timing services, and India's space capabilities.
